After some cleaning (cases, puctuations, whitespaces and stopwords), let's take a look at the distributions of word frequencies in these three corpora.

As we can see, most words don't appear that frequently. So what are the most frequent ones?

N50 N90
Blogs 942 12667

As we can see from the table above, with those most frequent ones, it takes 1202 words to cover 50% of all word instances in news while only 529 words are needed in tweets.
